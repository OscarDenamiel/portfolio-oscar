MY PROCESS

How I turn ambiguity into measurable outcomes.

01 Diagnose
Analyse before designing.

I audit the existing data first. If data gaps exist, I create them running targeted qual/quant research to generate the signal we need.

02 Scope
Frame the right problem.

Data tells me what's happening. Research tells me why. This is where I spend the most time, solving wrong problems at scale is expensive.

03 Solution
Design to the hypothesis

Mapping directly to a measurable hypothesis. No gut feelings, no assumptions. Just testable bets grounded in user evidence.

04 Validate
Experiment, measure, scale

I design for the experiment first. Small, fast, instrumented. Once a hypothesis is validated, I work with engineering to scale the final product.

MY APPROACH

How I create impact.

🚀 Growth & Experimentation

I don't believe in "gut feelings" or team assumptions. I design through a hypothesis-driven approach, utilizing A/B testing and multivariate experiments to optimize funnels. My goal is always to find the sweet spot where user friction decreases and business conversion rises.

🔎 Research-led strategy

Specialized in user research and data analysis. I turn complex data sets into actionable design artifacts. By practicing active listening and deep-dive discovery, I identify the "why" behind the numbers, ensuring we build the right solutions before building them right.

🤖 Innovation & efficiency

As an early adopter of AI-driven workflows, I build custom automation scripts and leverage emerging tech to speed up the design-to-development handoff and team alignment. I focus on building robust AI-Ready Design Systems that allow teams to scale without losing visual or functional consistency.
