I don't believe in "gut feelings" or team assumptions. I design through a hypothesis-driven approach, utilizing A/B testing and multivariate experiments to optimize funnels. My goal is always to find the sweet spot where user friction decreases and business conversion rises.

Specialized in user research and data analysis. I turn complex data sets into actionable design artifacts. By practicing active listening and deep-dive discovery, I identify the "why" behind the numbers, ensuring we build the right solutions before building them right.
